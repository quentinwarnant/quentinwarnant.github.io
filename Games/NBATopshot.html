NBA Top Shot

Role: Senior programmer

I developped the key shaders and rendering/exporting pipeline to generate assets that would later be sold as NFTs on the Top Shot online platform.

Description

Top Shot is an officially licensed NBA digital collectible platform. Match highlight clips, Player profiles and stats would be displayed on a digital collectible asset. These collectibles were also meant to play a role in the meta game of a NBA mobile game - but this element didn't release.
